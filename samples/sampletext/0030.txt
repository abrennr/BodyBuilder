  But I have already detained you too long.  My
friends, fellow-citizens, and countrymen, I take a re-
spectful leave of you. The time I have passed on
this side the Alleghany, has been a succession of
happy days. I have seen much to instruct, and much
to delight me.   I return you, again and again, my
unfeigned thanks, for the frankness and hospitality,
with which you have made me welcome; and
wherever I may go, or wherever I may be, I pray you
to believe I shall not lose the recollection of your
kindness.
